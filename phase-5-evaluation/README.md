# Phase 5 - Evaluation

## Overview

The **Evaluation phase** focuses on validating the selected model from both analytical and business perspectives, and determining whether it is ready for deployment.

Its purpose is to:
- validate model performance against predefined success criteria  
- assess expected business impact  
- review the execution of the ML process  
- make a formal decision regarding deployment or further iteration  

In this framework, Phase 5 acts as a **decision and governance layer**, ensuring that deployment is based on validated results, business alignment, and controlled evaluation.

---

## Key Outcomes

By the end of this phase, the project must have:

- Validated **model performance against success criteria**  
- Assessed and documented **expected business impact**  
- Completed **process review with identified gaps and improvements**  
- Produced a **formal decision on deployment or iteration**  

This ensures that deployment decisions are **evidence-based, transparent, and aligned with business objectives**.

## Deliverables in This Phase

### Governance Artifacts (Level G)

These documents provide formal validation and decision-making support.

- **P05_Model_Validation_Report_G**  
  Validates model performance against defined success criteria.

- **P05_Business_Impact_Assessment_G**  
  Assesses expected business value and operational impact.

- **P05_Process_Review_Report_G**  
  Reviews execution of the ML lifecycle and identifies gaps.

- **P05_Evaluation_Decision_Statement_G**  
  Documents the formal decision on whether to proceed to deployment or iterate.

---

## How This Phase Works

This phase formalizes evaluation and decision-making by:

- validating model performance against predefined success criteria  
- assessing whether the model delivers meaningful business value  
- reviewing the execution of the ML process for completeness and quality  
- consolidating findings into a formal decision  

The use of **governance artifacts (G)** ensures that:

- evaluation is structured and auditable  
- business impact is explicitly considered  
- decisions are formally documented and approved  

---

## Phase Gate

The phase is completed only after both conditions are met:

### 1. Outcome Validation

All key outcomes of the phase are achieved (see [**Key Outcomes**](#key-outcomes) section).

### 2. Deployment Decision Validation

- Model performance is validated against defined success criteria  
- Business impact is assessed and documented  
- Process review is completed with no critical gaps remaining  
- A formal decision is:
  - documented  
  - reviewed  
  - approved  

A formal decision is made to proceed to:  
[**Phase 6 - Deployment**](../phase-6-deployment/)

# Phase 6 - Deployment

## Overview

The **Deployment phase** focuses on implementing the approved solution in the production environment and ensuring its stable operation.

Its purpose is to:
- deploy model and supporting components into production  
- validate integration with existing systems and workflows  
- establish monitoring and alerting mechanisms  
- define maintenance and retraining strategy  
- complete final project documentation and knowledge transfer  

In this framework, Phase 6 acts as an **operational transition layer**, ensuring that the ML solution is successfully integrated into business processes and can be maintained over time.

---

## Key Outcomes

By the end of this phase, the project must have:

- Deployed **approved model and solution components** to production  
- Verified **production integration and operational workflows**  
- Established **monitoring and alerting mechanisms**  
- Documented **maintenance and retraining strategy**  
- Completed **final documentation and lessons learned**  
- Transferred **operational ownership**  

This ensures that the solution is **operational, maintainable, and supported in a production environment**.

## Deliverables in This Phase

### Governance Artifacts (Level G)

These documents define deployment decisions, monitoring strategy, and final reporting.

- **P06_Deployment_Plan_G**  
  Defines deployment approach, scope, and strategy.

- **P06_Deployment_Execution_Report_G**  
  Documents deployment execution and outcomes.

- **P06_Model_Monitoring_Framework_G**  
  Defines monitoring strategy, KPIs, and alerting approach.

- **P06_Final_Project_Report_G**  
  Consolidates project results, outcomes, and key findings.

- **P06_Lessons_Learned_G**  
  Captures insights, challenges, and recommendations for future projects.

---

### Technical Artifacts (Level T)

- **P06_Model_Operations_Runbook_T**  
  Defines operational procedures, support processes, and incident handling.

---

### Operational Artifacts

- **Deployed Model (Prod Instance)**  
  Production instance of the trained and approved model.

- **Model Monitoring Dashboard**  
  Provides visibility into model performance, data drift, and operational metrics.

- **Executive Presentation (optional)**  
  Summarizes results for executive stakeholders.

---

## How This Phase Works

This phase operationalizes the ML solution by:

- deploying validated model artifacts into production  
- integrating the solution into business workflows and systems  
- verifying end-to-end functionality and stability  
- establishing monitoring, alerting, and maintenance processes  
- transferring knowledge and responsibility to operational teams  

The combination of **governance, technical, and operational artifacts** ensures that:

- deployment is controlled and traceable  
- system performance is continuously monitored  
- operational teams are equipped to support the solution  

---

## Phase Gate

The phase is completed only after both conditions are met:

### 1. Outcome Validation

All key outcomes of the phase are achieved (see [**Key Outcomes**](#key-outcomes) section).

### 2. Operational Readiness Validation

- Approved solution components are deployed in the production environment  
- Production integration is verified and workflows are functioning as expected  
- Monitoring and alerting mechanisms are established  
- Maintenance and retraining strategy is documented  
- Final documentation and lessons learned are completed  

Operational ownership is formally transferred once all criteria are satisfied.
